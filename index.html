About Me

Professional Summary

I am a dedicated English Lecturer and Communication Skills Trainer with a strong commitment to academic excellence and student development. Over the years, I have cultivated an engaging, inclusive classroom environment where learners feel encouraged to explore language, literature, and ideas with confidence.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe education is not merely the transfer of knowledge, but the awakening of curiosity. My approach blends classic literary tradition with practical communication skills, empowering students to think critically, speak fluently, and write effectively for the modern world.

Hobbies & Interests

Beyond the classroom, I enjoy reading books, exploring places and travelling, and socialising — activities that keep my perspective fresh and my passion for storytelling alive.
